About Me

Hi, I'm Talia, the photographer behind Meraki Photography. I'm a college student who loves taking photographs, from landscapes to fine detail I love taking photos of many different things and plan to continue to do so far into the future. I enjoy spending the evenings after a photoshoot choosing the best shots and sometimes playing around on photoshop trying out different edits. Through photography I hope to make everyone who views each photo to take a moment to think, whether it's of a past memory, a future dream or just curiosity in the moment. Take a look at my portfolio to see a selected group of my photographs and go check out my instagram @MerakiPhotography for the full range!
